About Dx

Founded in 2020 in response to DeFi fraud, Dx has evolved into a powerhouse platform. Their flagship products include DxMint for secure tokens, DxSale for easy crowdsales, and DxLock for automatic fund locking. Rebranded as Dx, they aspire to be the "Apple of Web 3.0," with a focus on NFTs and gaming. The $ SALE token is utilized for platform utility and governance, while they actively seek partners for ecosystem expansion.

What sets Dx apart

Decentralized Launchpad: Dx is a pioneer in decentralized launchpads.

One Token, One Supply, Infinite Chains: Seamless interaction with a token across different blockchains.

Supported Networks: Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, and Polygon are flexibly supported.

Dedicated Team: Dx shines with a highly motivated and experienced team.

This partnership opens doors for us and our clients to access innovative DeFi solutions. Together with Dx, we are shaping the future of decentralized finance. We are confident that this collaboration will bear fruit and look forward to diving into the exciting world of Dx with you.
